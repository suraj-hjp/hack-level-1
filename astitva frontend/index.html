The most popular pencil of india

Hindustan Pencils is India's largest pencil manufacturer with high-quality products beiing delevered to over 50 countries worldwide. Our State-of-the-art maufacturing units with the latests machinery set up daily produce 8.5 million pencils, 1.7 million sharpeners, 2.7 million erasers, 0.3 million scales, 1 million pens.

Nataraj, the first brand of Hindustan Pencils was launched with a brand philosophy of pencils for all, thus carrying a value for money image.

Apsara, launched in 1970s as a premium stationery brand started with drawing pencils as its first product. With the launch of writing pencils and other allied stationery products in 1990’s, Apsara has grown to be the most preferred scholastic brand.

Hindustan Pencils started manufacturing pens in the year 2007. Nataraj Classic Ball Pen, with its famous Nataraj red and black design is now one of the most visible pens in India.
